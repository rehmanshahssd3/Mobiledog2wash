Services

Every visit, top to tail.

Pick a single service or bundle the full spa treatment — everything happens curbside, in view, in one visit.

Warm Wash & Rinse

Dermatologist-tested shampoo, hand-massaged in, then rinsed with continuously heated water.

Blow-Dry & Brush-Out

High-velocity dryer plus de-shedding brush to cut loose fur and leave the coat soft.

Nail Trim & Paw Care

Gentle nail trimming and paw-pad conditioning, done calmly with plenty of breaks.

De-Shed Treatment

Deep-coat shed control for double-coated breeds — a favorite before shedding season.

Flea & Tick Treatment

Vet-approved treatment applied after the wash, with a full rinse-through check.

Large-Breed Add-On

Extra time, extra towels, extra patience — built specifically for 70lb+ dogs.
